About Me

With four years of experience as a Machine Learning Engineer and Lecturer in Ghana's Bono Region, I built and implemented network-based recognition solutions utilizing Microsoft Azure AI tools (Vision, Document Intelligence, Open AI) for automation and document processing.

Published peer-reviewed research in AI/Computer vision, oversaw Azure-based data annotation pipelines, and led cross-functional teams in creating scalable ML models for web/mobile platforms.
